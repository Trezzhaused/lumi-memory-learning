# LUMI Acceptable Use Policy

Effective: July 2026

LUMI is a sovereign AI platform intended for
constructive, lawful, and ethical use.

## Prohibited Uses

You may NOT use LUMI to:

1. Develop or deploy weapons systems
2. Generate bioweapons, chemical weapons, or related research
3. Create or distribute malware, ransomware, or spyware
4. Conduct mass surveillance or unlawful tracking
5. Generate non-consensual intimate imagery (deepfakes)
6. Circumvent safety, audit, or TEE protections
7. Violate export control or sanctions regulations
8. Enable autonomous lethal systems

## Enforcement

Violations may result in termination of access,
refusal of support, and referral to authorities.

## Reporting

Report violations to:
abuse@lumi-ai.dev

## Commitment

LUMI exists to expand human capability safely.
We reserve the right to deny use to any party
engaged in prohibited activities.
