# LUMI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

## 1. Data Collection

LUMI is designed for local and sovereign deployment.
By default, LUMI does not collect, transmit, or store
personally identifiable information (PII).

## 2. Telemetry

No telemetry is collected unless explicitly enabled by the user.

## 3. Data Storage

All data remains on the user's infrastructure.
Cloud deployment options include optional encrypted backups.

## 4. Third Parties

LUMI does not share data with third parties
unless required by law.

## 5. Security

LUMI supports TEE and HSM-backed audit logging
to ensure data integrity and confidentiality.

## 6. User Rights

Users may request deletion of any data stored
within their own deployment environment.
